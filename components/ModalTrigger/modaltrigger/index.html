Modal Trigger

Add dialogs to your site for lightboxes, user notifications, or completely custom content.

Example#

Simple modal trigger without any aditional props. Click the button below to try it.

<ModalTrigger
trigger={<Button>Click me to show modal</Button>}
content={() => <p>Show your content here</p>}
/>

Title#

Show title on the header section of the modal with title prop.

<ModalTrigger
trigger={<Button>Click me to show modal</Button>}
content={() => <p>Show your content here</p>}
title="Title" isAnimate
/>

Backdrop#

Make the modal not closeable when clicking outside it by setting backdrop to 'static'.

<ModalTrigger
trigger={<Button>Click me to show modal</Button>}
content={() => <p>Show your content here</p>}
backDrop="static" isAnimate
/>

Padding#

Make the padding less by setting paddingContent to 'less-padding'.

<ModalTrigger
trigger={<Button>Click me to show modal</Button>}
content={() => <p>Show your content here</p>}
paddingContent="less-padding" isAnimate
/>

Hide header#

Hide the header section of the modal with hideHeader prop.

<ModalTrigger
trigger={<Button>Click me to show modal</Button>}
content={() => <p>Show your content here</p>}
hideHeader isAnimate
/>

API#

import ModalTrigger from "erxes-ui/lib/components/ModalTrigger";
