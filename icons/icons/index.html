Erxes-icon

Icons#

Icons are an amazing tool for building layouts, webs and apps that are quicker to interact with. It allows us to use a little less text than we normally would needed and helps people to navigate through our designs quicker. Erxes-icon is a set of open-source neutral-style system symbols elaborately crafted for designers and developers. Search and choose icon for your purpose. Click to copy icon name.
